Machine learning for univariate time-series

Various time-series forecasting algorithms for 1-D data in python. In particular, the algorithms implemented/compared are: AR(I), Ridge Regression, Lasso Regression, RandomForestRegressor and LSTM.

An Agent-Based Ramsey-Cass-Koopmans model with Social Learning

In this project with the Potsdam Institute for Climate Impact Research, I extended a basic macroeconomic growth model to an agent-based model with social dynamics. This yields surprising results and at the same time alleviates some of the problems of normal representative agent models.

Simulation and Analysis of a SEIR epidemic on a Temporal Network

In this project, I investigate the use of a temporally resolved network dataset of primary school children's contact patterns to model an influenza-like (SEIR) outbreak in a school. I partly reproduce and analyze the effects of different simulated interventions to prevent outbreaks.

Fast Linear Solvers in C++

This project's purpose was to analyze the different scaling patterns of commonly used Linear solvers. The algorithms implemented here are: LU, QR (implicit and explicit), SD, CG, CG with Jacobi pre-conditioning, Sucessive-overrelaxation, Jacobi, Gauss-Seidel.

Numerics of Electrochemistry

This project explores mathematically modeling electrochemical reaction taking place at the surface of an electrode. Chronoamperometry and voltametry experiments are numerically investigated with forward- and backward Euler and show high accuracy.
